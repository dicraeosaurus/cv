Details of PhD project

I am a PhD candidate at the Museum für Naturkunde, Berlin where I work on Dicraeosaurid sauropods.
My research specifically focuses on the systematics and biomechanics of Dicraeosaurus hansemanni, a
sauropod dinosaur from the Late Jurassic of Tendaguru, Tanzania.
